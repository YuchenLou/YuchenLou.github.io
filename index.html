About

Hi! I am Yuchen Lou (楼昱辰), a Ph.D candidate in IEMS at Northwestern University from 2021 to 2026 (expected). I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Jorge Nocedal and Prof. Andreas Waechter.

My current research interests are optimization theories, algorithms, and their applications in deep learning. I am also interested in connecting optimization with any other fields or practical problems.

Before I came to Northwestern, I completed my Bachelor's degree in Mathematics from The University of Hong Kong (HKU) in 2021, where I was supervised by Prof. Xiaoming Yuan and Prof. Zheng Qu. I also spent a year working with Prof. Wotao Yin, Prof. Daniel McKenzie, and Prof. HanQin Cai from the Department of Mathematics at UCLA.
